How it all started

In 2018, Jason Brown began breaking down games for high school teams around the bay area. Shortly after, he noticed the lack of video analytics and insights at the high school and junior college levels. With his expertise in sports performance softwares, sports dynamics, and experience working with elite sports organizations, Jason knew he could change the way teams watch film and understand themselves, leading to the improvement of team performance.

​By the beginning of 2020, Jason was working with several sports teams. To help carry out his mission, Jason reached out to an old friend, Lucas Cortez, who brought experience ranging from the high school to professional level. Together, Jason and Lucas have created a unique service where teams can work with professional sports analysts to gain a competitive advantage.
